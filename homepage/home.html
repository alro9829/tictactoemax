Rules

1. The game is played on a 20-by-20 square grid.
2. You are assigned a random color, boxes you mark are assigned this color.
3. The first player to get 5 marked boxes in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.
4. Another player can "steal" a box you had already marked
5. There is a 2 second cooldown between each click
